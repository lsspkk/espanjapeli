SCENE DESCRIPTION GUIDELINES
================================================================================

FORMAT FOR EACH IDEA:

```
FILE: [filename].svg
PHRASE: [Spanish phrase] ([English translation])

SCENE TITLE: "[A catchy, descriptive title]"

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
[2-3 paragraphs describing the scene in detail]

KEY ELEMENTS:
- Character(s): [Who is in the scene, their poses, expressions]
- Setting: [Where the scene takes place]
- Main Action: [What is happening - this should be OBVIOUS in 1 second]
- Props: [1-3 specific objects that reinforce the phrase meaning]
- Mood/Atmosphere: [Colors, lighting, emotional tone]

EDUCATIONAL CLARITY:
[1-2 sentences explaining how this image teaches the Spanish phrase]

SIGNATURE:
[Note: The finished SVG must include a small AI model signature in the bottom 
right corner (e.g., "Composer 1", "Gemini 3 Pro", etc.) - this will be added 
by the illustrator, not described in detail here]
```

DESCRIPTION WRITING RULES:

1. BE SPECIFIC & VISUAL
   BAD: "Peppa looks happy"
   GOOD: "Peppa has her eyes squeezed shut in joy, mouth wide open in a huge grin,
          with rosy pink cheeks and both trotters thrown up in the air"

2. ONE CLEAR STORY IN ONE SECOND
   - A child should understand the scene instantly
   - One main action, not multiple competing activities
   - The phrase meaning should be visually obvious

3. USE THE PEPPA PIG AESTHETIC
   - Simple, rounded shapes
   - Bright, cheerful colors
   - Characters facing slightly to the side (the classic Peppa pose)
   - Big heads, small bodies
   - Expressive faces with minimal features

4. MAKE IT KID-DELIGHTFUL
   - Include elements children love (muddy puddles, dinosaurs, birthday cake)
   - Show friendship, family warmth, simple joys
   - Keep it safe and gentle (no scary elements)
   - Add small delightful details (a butterfly, a rainbow, sparkles)

5. KEEP BACKGROUNDS SIMPLE
   - One or two large shapes (sky, grass, floor, wall)
   - Soft gradients or solid colors
   - Don't overload with tiny details
   - Leave breathing room around characters

6. DESCRIBE COLORS EXPLICITLY
   - "A bright red ball" not just "a ball"
   - "Soft blue sky with fluffy white clouds"
   - "Warm golden sunlight streaming through the window"

7. INDICATE RELATIVE POSITIONS
   - "Peppa stands in the center, slightly left"
   - "George peeks from behind a tree on the right"
   - "A rainbow arcs across the upper portion of the scene"

EXAMPLE 1 - TIME/DAILY ROUTINE:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FILE: 104_its_morning.svg
PHRASE: Es por la mañana. (It's morning.)

SCENE TITLE: "The Sunrise Awakening"

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
A cozy bedroom scene with warm morning light streaming through partially open
curtains. Through the window, we see a beautiful sunrise - the sun is a soft
orange-yellow circle just peeking over gentle hills, with the sky transitioning
from peachy-pink near the horizon to soft blue above. Two little birds sit on
a tree branch outside, with small musical notes floating near them to show
they're singing the morning chorus.

Inside, Peppa sits up in her bed with rumpled pink covers, her arms stretching
high above her head in a big morning stretch. Her eyes are just opening (drawn
as happy curved lines), and she has a peaceful, content expression. On her
nightstand, an alarm clock shows 7:00, and her teddy bear "Teddy" lies beside
her pillow. A fuzzy pair of slippers waits neatly by the bed.

KEY ELEMENTS:
- Character(s): Peppa sitting up in bed, stretching with arms raised, sleepy smile
- Setting: Peppa's bedroom with window view of sunrise
- Main Action: Peppa waking up and stretching
- Props: Alarm clock (7:00), teddy bear, morning birds outside, slippers
- Mood/Atmosphere: Warm golden-pink morning light, peaceful and fresh

EDUCATIONAL CLARITY:
The sunrise, alarm clock time, and stretching Peppa clearly convey "morning"
to young learners - this is unmistakably an early-day scene.

EXAMPLE 2 - EMOTION/EXPRESSION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FILE: 88_how_funny.svg
PHRASE: ¡Qué risa! (How funny!)

SCENE TITLE: "Peppa's Giggle Fit"

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
Peppa is caught mid-laugh, doubled over with both trotters clutching her belly.
Her whole body shakes with laughter - indicated by small motion lines around
her. Her eyes are squeezed completely shut, and her mouth is wide open showing
pure joy. Tiny tears of laughter appear at the corners of her eyes (just small
sparkle shapes).

Around her head, colorful text reads "JA JA JA" in playful bubble letters -
red, yellow, and blue. The background is a simple, cheerful yellow-to-orange
gradient that radiates the warmth of laughter. Small star sparkles float
around Peppa to emphasize the joy of the moment.

KEY ELEMENTS:
- Character(s): Peppa bent over laughing, holding belly, eyes shut, mouth wide
- Setting: Simple warm gradient background (no specific location needed)
- Main Action: Peppa laughing uncontrollably
- Props: "JA JA JA" text, sparkle stars, motion lines
- Mood/Atmosphere: Bright, warm yellows and oranges, pure joy

EDUCATIONAL CLARITY:
The exaggerated laughing pose and "JA JA JA" text immediately communicate
"funny/laughter" - perfect for teaching this expression.

EXAMPLE 3 - ACTIVITY/INVITATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FILE: 81_lets_play_together.svg
PHRASE: ¡Vamos a jugar juntos! (Let's play together!)

SCENE TITLE: "The Invitation to Play"

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
Peppa stands in the center of a sunny playground, her arms spread wide open
in a welcoming gesture. Her face beams with an excited, inviting smile. She's
positioned as if she's just called out to friends to come join her.

Arranged in a loose semicircle around her feet are several colorful toys: a
bright red bouncy ball, a jump rope with blue and yellow handles, and a stack
of rainbow-colored building blocks. The playground behind her shows simple
shapes suggesting a slide (in cheerful blue) and swing set.

The sky is bright blue with two puffy white clouds. Soft green grass covers
the ground with a few tiny flowers dotted about.

KEY ELEMENTS:
- Character(s): Peppa with arms wide open, inviting expression, standing central
- Setting: Sunny playground with slide and swings in background
- Main Action: Peppa inviting others to play (welcoming gesture)
- Props: Ball, jump rope, building blocks - variety suggests "play"
- Mood/Atmosphere: Bright, sunny, welcoming, energetic

EDUCATIONAL CLARITY:
The open-armed invitation pose combined with scattered toys clearly shows
"let's play together" - Peppa is welcoming friends to join in play.

NOTES FOR THE AI ILLUSTRATOR
================================================================================

When these descriptions are passed to the drawing agent, remind them:
1. Maintain Peppa Pig visual style (simple shapes, friendly faces)
2. Use the exact character colors from the reference
3. Keep SVGs lightweight (prefer simple shapes over complex paths)
4. Test readability at small sizes
5. Make the main action obvious at a glance
6. Use gentle gradients sparingly, prefer flat colors
7. Include the phrase context visually without needing text labels
8. **REQUIRED: Add AI model signature in bottom right corner** - Include a small 
   text element in the bottom right corner (around x="390" y="390") with the 
   AI model name used to create the image, for example "Composer 1", "Gemini 3 Pro", 
   or "GPT 5.2". Use small font size (10-12px), right-aligned text, and subtle 
   color (#5B6470 with opacity 0.7-0.85) so it doesn't distract from the main image.
